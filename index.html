About The IT Society

The IT Society is an academic and social group aimed at supporting students who are taking a computing-based course or are interested in learning about IT. We run sessions covering all disciplines, from coding to cyber security to networking, we have something for everyone. We meet every Thursday in the FTC building and have bi-weekly socials on a Friday. In our sessions, we run a number of workshops, talks, and events throughout the year.

Regular Activities

  • Hackathons
  • Capture the flag
  • Talks from industry
  • Support sessions
  • Learning a skill night
